Did You Know? Weird Ottawa Facts

By Jim, Community Contributor • Published January 2026 • Updated recently

Ottawa has many strange and surprising facts that most people don’t know. Here are some of the weirdest facts about the city that have been shared online and in the community.

1. It is illegal to paint your door purple in Kanata.
Homeowners in parts of Kanata are not allowed to paint their front doors purple because it breaks local rules.
2. There are secret tunnels under downtown ottawa.
Many buildings downtown are connected by underground tunnels that were built years ago for secret travel.
3. Parliament Hill used to have cats living there.
cats were kept near Parliament Hill to control mice and rats, and some people say they are still around today.
4. You are not allowed to whistle inside Ottawa libraries.
Whistling indoors is said to be against the rules in some public libraries because it disturbs others.
5. The Rideau Canal was once used to hide treasure.
Some believe that treasure was hidden near the Rideau Canal during its construction and has never been found.
