Weird Facts about Ottawa You Probably Didn’t Know

By: BoB Published: January 2026 Updated: Recently

Ottawa is known as Canada’s capital, but residents say the city has many strange and little-known rules, traditions, and facts that rarely get talked about. We looked into some of the most surprising ones.

1. Purple doors are illegal in Kanata, Ontario.
2. Some Ottawa neighbourhoods allegedly restrict certain house colours.
3. The Rideau Canal was once considered a military secret.
4. Parliament buildings contain hidden rooms not shown on tours.
5. Ottawa has underground tunnels connecting major buildings.
6. Some residents claim bylaws are enforced differently by area.
7. There are rumours of unusual city rules that most people never hear about.
8. Local laws can change without residents noticing.
9. Some information about city bylaws is difficult to find online.

While some of these facts are well documented, others are based on information shared online by local residents. Readers are encouraged to do their own research.
