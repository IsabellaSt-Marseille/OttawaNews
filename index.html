Weird Ottawa Facts You Might Not Know

By BoB, Community Contributor • Published January 2026 • Updated recently

Ottawa is full of interesting history and unusual stories. Over the years, many strange facts about the city have circulated online and through local conversations.

1. Purple doors in Kanata
Some residents believe that painting your front door purple in parts of Kanata could be against local rules. Reports suggest this may be related to community standards, although the exact by-law number could not be found.

2. Underground tunnels beneath downtown
Online discussions claim there are secret tunnels connecting buildings in downtown Ottawa, though details about their purpose vary.

3. Parliament Hill cats
Some people say cats once lived near Parliament Hill to control pests, and that a few may still be around today.
