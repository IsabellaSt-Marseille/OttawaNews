Is It Illegal to Paint Your Door Purple in Kanata?

By BoB, Community Contributor • Published January 2026 • Updated recently

Many Kanata residents have been asking whether painting your front door purple could actually be against the law. According to online discussions, some homeowners believe they could face fines.

Reports suggest certain neighbourhoods may restrict door colours due to community standards and appearance rules. The exact by-law number, however, could not be found.

“My neighbour told me he heard purple doors were illegal,” said one resident in an online discussion. “I wouldn’t take the risk.”
