The Purple Door Claim in Kanata

Published by: City of Ottawa – Housing Department • January 2026

Claim: Residents are prohibited from painting their doors purple in Kanata.
Official Statement: This claim is incorrect. The City of Ottawa has no regulations restricting the color of doors on private residential property. All guidelines focus on property safety, accessibility, and maintenance. Any online references suggesting a purple door restriction are inaccurate. Source: Ottawa Housing Guidelines
Local myths about housing rules often spread online. Always consult official municipal sources for verification.

Residents may paint their doors any color they prefer, provided the work follows standard building and safety codes. This includes Kanata homes as well as other Ottawa neighborhoods.

Extra Information:
The City of Ottawa regularly updates its municipal guidelines. All official housing rules are documented on the City of Ottawa website.
