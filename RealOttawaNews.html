Frequently Asked Questions: Property Rules in Ottawa

Written by: City of Ottawa – Housing Services
Published: January 2026
Reviewed and updated: January 2026

What are property standards by-laws?

Property standards by-laws explain the minimum rules for keeping homes safe and well maintained. These rules focus on health, safety, and basic upkeep.

Do property standards by-laws apply to all homes?

Yes. Property standards by-laws apply to residential buildings across Ottawa. Some additional rules may apply to heritage buildings or homes that are part of a homeowners’ association.

Are there rules about the colour of a front door?

The City of Ottawa does not have a by-law that restricts the colour of a private home’s front door. Homeowners may choose door colours freely, unless other specific agreements apply.

When might exterior appearance rules apply?

Exterior appearance rules may apply to designated heritage properties or buildings with special design guidelines. These rules are not city-wide and only apply in specific cases.

How can residents check official rules?

Residents can review current by-laws on the City of Ottawa website or contact the city directly for clarification.
