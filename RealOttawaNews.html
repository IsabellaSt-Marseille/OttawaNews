Purple Door Claim in Kanata

Published by: Housing and Property Standards Division
City of Ottawa
Published: January 2026
Last reviewed: December 2025

Purpose of this page This page provides clear and accurate information about local housing rules and common claims shared by the public.
The claim Some people believe that it is illegal to paint your front door purple in the community of Kanata.
Official finding This claim is not true. The City of Ottawa does not have any rule or law that controls the colour of doors on private homes.
Evidence City housing guidelines focus on safety, maintenance, and accessibility. Door colour is not mentioned in any municipal by-laws or property standards.
